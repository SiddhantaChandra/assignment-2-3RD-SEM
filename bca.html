Computer Application

University of Engineering and Management, Kolkata provides BCA degree. The curriculum follow a hands-on practical approach of software development to grow an understanding of the IT industry.

The course is three years long, and students who are enrolled are taught various of topics from basic to advanced programing, programing languages such as C, C++, Java and Python.
